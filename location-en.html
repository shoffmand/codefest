Location

Carleton University, in Ottawa, Ontario
1125 Colonel By Drive, Ottawa, ON

Activities will take place in Southam Hall (SA) and the Human Computer Interaction building (HC). Both are near the Colonel By entrance.

We encourage participants to walk, bike or take public transit to get there. Parking is also available.

Bike racks

Bike racks are available across the Carleton University campus.

Public transit to and from Carleton University

Carleton University is served by the following OCTranspo routes:

  • O-Train
  • Route 4
  • Route 7
  • Route 111

Parking

Parking Lot #1 (4-hour maximum) on Library Road. There are other parking lots across the Carleton University campus.

Parking can be paid using either the pay and display machines or the text-to-park service iParked.ca. The parking rate is $1.75/half-hour.

Visit the Carleton University Parking services page for more information.

Places to get food

Food and drinks are not provided. So bring your lunch and something to drink. Water fountains are available on campus.

Food and drink are forbidden in the Kailash Mital Theatre.

You can also purchase food and drinks on campus:

  • Main Food Court (2nd floor University Centre) – Thursday: 10:30am–5:00pm and Friday: 10:30am–3:00pm
  • PageBreak Café (MacOdrum Library) – Thursday: 8:00am–7:00pm and Friday: 8:00am–4:00pm
  • Tim Hortons (1st floor University Centre) – Thursday/Friday: 7:30am–6:00pm
